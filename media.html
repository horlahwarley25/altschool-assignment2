About Me

Hello! My name is Moses Olawale Ajayi. I'm passionate about technology and shaping the future. Here's a bit about me:

Early Life and Education

Born on June 25th, 1999, in Lagos, Nigeria, Moses grew up with a fascination for technology. He attended Barth Academy for primary school and Matvot College for secondary school, graduating in 2017. He is currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at Yaba College of Technology.

Career Path

Before diving into higher education, Moses gained hands-on experience in iPhone hardware repair in Ikeja, Lagos. This practical experience shaped his technical skills and fueled his passion for technology. In 2024, Moses joined AltSchool Africa to specialize in frontend engineering, aiming to master web development and create engaging user interfaces.

Favorite Hobby: Gaming

Personal Life

Moses comes from a supportive family, including his parents and two brothers. When not studying or working on technical projects, he enjoys playing FIFA on PlayStation and playing football.

Looking Ahead

Moses is driven by the belief that "what will be will be." He embraces life's challenges and opportunities with grace and acceptance, always striving to grow and achieve his goals in the tech industry.
